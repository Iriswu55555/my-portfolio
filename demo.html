Segmenting Youth Behavior for Internet Usage

Python (Pandas, Scikit-learn) · K-means Clustering · Random Forest

Objective

Identify behavioral clusters among 6,000+ K–12 students to support national internet safety policy development.

My Role

  • Conducted cluster analysis to identify 4 distinct digital behavior groups
  • Flagged high-risk group with excessive screen time and low academic engagement
  • Designed early intervention model adopted by educational policy advisors

Outcomes

Post-launch surveys showed a 30% increase in compliance knowledge and a 98% module completion rate within the first quarter. The module was later adapted for contractor onboarding.
