ISO Compliance Onboarding Training

Instructional Design · Compliance Training · ADDIE Model

Overview

This onboarding training module was created for a global manufacturing firm to ensure new employees understand ISO 9001, 14001, and 45001 requirements. I led the full instructional design process using the ADDIE framework, collaborating with subject matter experts and stakeholders.

My Role

  • Conducted training needs analysis
  • Storyboarded and developed slide decks, quizzes, and facilitator guides
  • Integrated training into internal LMS (SCORM-compliant)
  • Localized materials into three languages

Outcomes

Post-launch surveys indicated a 30% increase in compliance knowledge and a 98% completion rate within the first quarter. The module was later adapted for contractor onboarding.
